About Looking4Group

I created this website because I, a married man in my 30's, wanted to play some Pokemon TCG. I got some newe sweet cards but didn't know any cool kids, who still played Pokemon in their 30's. What if I could broadcast in some way that I wanted to play Pokemon TCG at a local Starbucks? That would be AWESOME! Maybe I would make a few friends in the process. I made this dream a reality. Hopefully, you will enjoy meeting new gamers, develope some rivalries, and become a better a gamer.
